WELCOME TO HOSTEL ROOM ALLOTMENT



Hostel Room Selection Site:  is a GUI based room allotment process where one can go through the map and structure of the inner hostel design and select the room as per his interest and convenience.

Instructions for Student to keep in mind while going through Room selection Process

  • This is one time navigation webpage

  • Do not try to refresh or press "Back" button from any existing

  • If at any time submit button is pressed then informations filled won't be allowed to revert back as his/her data will be fed on database and he/she might have to loose out on his/her desired room

  • Once room alloted cannot be changed

  • Go through every page sincerely and make sure all the required informations are filled properly

    • System Objectives

    • Gives new student idea about their residing place using 3d walkway design
    • Helps in managing hostel by having functionalities of Rooms Status & Student information
    • Provide a common webpage for various students
    • Create a transparent database of scholars
    • Avoid duplication in processing
    • Harmonisation of different Scholarships schemes & norms
    • Application of Direct Benefit Transfer

    Benefits

    1: Student can find his room easily using 3d walkway design.

    2: All students information available under one umbrella.

    3: Single integrated application for all students

    4: Easily any students information can be fetch and status of Rooms too, thus helps in managing hostel
